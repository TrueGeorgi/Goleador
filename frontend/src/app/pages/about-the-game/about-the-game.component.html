How to Play Goleador

🏆 Your Team

When you create an account, you get a team of 15 players, each with a skill level between 0 and 20 and a designated position.

📈 Training Your Players

Train your players to boost their skills and maximize their potential. Training costs money—the higher the skill level, the more expensive it becomes.

⚽ Playing a Match

Click "Play a Game" to be matched with an opponent automatically. Your team’s performance depends on:

  • Attackers – Key for scoring goals
  • Midfielders – Support both attack and defense
  • Defenders & Goalkeeper – Essential for preventing goals

📊 Scoring System

  • Win → +3 points
  • Loss → -3 points
  • Draw → +1 point each

Train your squad, strategize wisely, and aim for the top of the leaderboard. Good luck, coach!
